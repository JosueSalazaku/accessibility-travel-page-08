DESTINATION HIGHLIGHTS & TRAVEL TIPS


Ayutthaya, Thailand
Historical Marvels and Culinary Delights. Explore ancient temples like Wat Mahathat and hire a local guide for historical context. Dive into Ayutthaya's renowned street food scene; try boat noodles, river prawns, and Khanom Krok.

Kalandula Falls, Angola
Nature's Majestic Masterpiece. Embark on a guided hike for the best views. Bring comfortable footwear, a camera, binoculars for birdwatching, and insect repellent.

Boston, USA
Historical Treasures and Culinary Diversity. Stroll the Freedom Trail and consider a Boston CityPASS for discounts. Enjoy diverse culinary offerings, from seafood at Quincy Market to famous lobster rolls.

Machu Picchu, Peru
Lost City of the Incas. Marvel at the ancient marvel of Machu Picchu, an iconic Inca archaeological site in the Andes.Plan ahead for limited daily permits, acclimatize in Cusco, and consider hiring a local guide for insights.


Berat, Albania
City of a Thousand Windows Stay in a traditional guesthouse in the old town for the full experience. Immerse in unique Albanian cuisine with dishes like Fergese. Visit local markets for fresh produce.

Nagasaki, Tsushima
Japanese History and Fusion Cuisine Engage a local guide for insights into historical sites. Don't miss the local specialty, champon. Learn basic Japanese phrases for interactions.

Yerevan, Armenia
Ancient Wonders and Bold Flavors Explore Erebuni Fortress and take in eclectic architecture. Indulge in Armenian cuisine; try dolma, lavash, and khorovats. Visit local markets for traditional spices.

Serra da Estrela, Portugal
Mountain Escape with Culinary Charms Explore hiking trails and consider a guided nature tour. Sample Queijo da Serra cheese and visit local markets. Check weather conditions before heading out.
